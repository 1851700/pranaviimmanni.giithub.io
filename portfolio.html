Scratch Project

This game is called Flappy . The objective is to pass through as many green obstacles without hitting them or falling. If you do end up falling or hitting the green obstacels, the game will be over and you must restart. The more obstacles you pass, the higher your score gets!


Project 4.1.4

In this project, we have created a stimulation called "simple economy" where the model will be stimulating economic exchange where each person gives someone else a $1 and either having to gain or lose the money. Each green dot will represent an agent. The agent will move to the left if they lose money, and move to the right if they gain money.
